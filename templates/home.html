Asynchronous Banking Transaction System Showcase

A demonstration of a Flask-based banking platform that features multithreaded transaction processing, SQLite persistence, and "worker-queue" architecture.

Concurrency

Multithreading is used to process all transactions asynchronously through a "worker-queue" task system.

SQLite Usage

Data is stored via Python's SQLite3 allowing persistent logging to provide a complete account history and audit tracking.

Where Flask Comes Into Play

Flask routes integrate with a backend service dedicated to transactions for clear seperation of work.
